1. Our approach

Factory 2 Hair & Home respects your privacy and handles personal information in line with the Privacy Act 2020. This placeholder policy explains what we collect and why, and should be reviewed before the site goes live.

2. What we collect

When you make an enquiry or booking we may collect your name, phone number, email address and the details you include about the service you'd like. If you leave private feedback, we collect your name, email, rating and comments.

3. How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and confirm appointments
  • To contact you about your booking or feedback
  • To improve the service we offer in the salon

We don't sell your information, and we don't send marketing you didn't ask for.

4. Forms & third parties

Our enquiry and feedback forms are delivered by FormSubmit, which processes the message so it reaches our inbox. Their handling of that data is governed by their own privacy terms. We only share your information where it's needed to provide our service or where the law requires it.

5. Keeping it safe

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share and keep it only as long as we need it for the purpose it was given.

6. Your rights

You can ask to see the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to correct it. Just get in touch and we'll help.
