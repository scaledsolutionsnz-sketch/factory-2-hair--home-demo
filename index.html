About the salon

A little pocket of calm on the Invercargill high street.

Factory 2 Hair & Home is a small, personal salon — the kind where the same stylist remembers your last colour and asks how the holiday went. We keep the numbers low so nobody's ever waiting on a rushed chair.

The "& Home" side is exactly that: a curated corner of candles and homeware we love, so you can take a little of the salon feeling with you when you leave.

  • Locally owned and run in Invercargill
  • A proper consult before every cut or colour
  • Honest advice — no upsell you didn't ask for
